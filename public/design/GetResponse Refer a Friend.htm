How does it work?

STEP 1

Invite friends and contacts to check out GetResponse by sending an email, posting on social media, or pasting your personalized link on your blog or website.

STEP 2

If someone clicks your link and buys a GetResponse account, both of you receive a $30 credit to apply toward future payments!

STEP 3

To check out your referred accounts and earned credits, visit the Refer a Friend tab in your account. You can redeem the credits and apply them to an upcoming payment whenever you feel like!

STEP 4

Refer 3 friends who sign up as paid customers and get your free certification course voucher. Save more while becoming an online marketing expert.

Frequently Asked Questions

Any GetResponse customer with a paid account can participate
in the Referral Program.

  • Who can join the Referral Program?

    Any GetResponse customer with a paid account can participate in the Referral Program. Dont have an account? Set one up here.

  • Where can I find my personalized link?

    Log in to your account and navigate to the Refer a friend area

  • How many friends can I refer?

    As many as you like! Theres no limit on how many friends you refer. But there is a cap on how many credits you receive each year.

  • How much credit can I earn?

    You can get up to $480 worth of credits per year, regardless of how many people you refer.

  • How do I get my free online certification course voucher?

    Youll be eligible once 3 of your friends become paid users. It can take up to 30 days to verify your referrals. Well then send you a voucher, and set up your certification account.

  • Ive already invited 3 or more paid accounts. Can I still get a reward?

    If you referred friends before this offer, youll need to invite another 3 or more paid accounts to get the reward. You can only redeem one certification voucher with this offer.

  • How can I redeem my credits?

    Go to the Refer a friend area in your account and click Redeem credits. Your credits will be applied to your next bill.
